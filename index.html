About Us

"Quality is our first priority"

With a dedication to product quality and excellent customer service, Galaxy Sewing Machine Industries is committed
to an “At Your Side philosophy with its customers, business partners and colleagues”.
Established in April 1988 in Delhi, India.
All the products of Galaxy Sewing Machine have always been of the best quality which has helped the
company to grow throughout the glob with its numerous branches and variety of sewing machine models.
Galaxy Sewing Machine has a range from traditional to trendy models and domestic to industrial models.
Providing after sale service is being the primary objective and informing about new product updates being the
secondary. Inferior or defective products are rejected at the testing phase and destroyed then and there only. This
company has always tried to provide top quality products to their clients and always have provided as per customer requirement.
